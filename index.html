About

This is the main website of Zhen Yang (杨镇). I got my B.Sc. in Physics from Nanjing University in 2021, and was a visiting student at Duke University in 2019. My undergraduate research mainly focuses on percolation, glass and jamming transitions — a common theme is to study the effect of disorder on system’s structure and dynamics. My current research interests cover statistical physics (especially theory of phase transitions and critical phenomena), condensed matter physics, and complex systems. I am also open to other fields in and outside physics.

Apart from research, I love reading books related to history and listening to music, mostly piano solos (classical & folk). I also taught myself to play my favorite music on the piano.
